### Shopping-List-Budget-Matcher
The "Shopping List Budget Matcher" is a solution designed to streamline the shopping experience  by empowering customers to curate  their listwithin budgetary constraints.  By fostering satisfaction and efficiency, it enhances the shopping experience and boosts supermarket sales
